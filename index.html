What is Science Olympiad?

"Founded in 1984, Science Olympiad is the premier team STEM competition in the nation, providing standards-based challenges to 6,000 teams at 425 tournaments in all 50 states"

- Science Olympiad Inc.




Teams consist of 15 students who compete in groups of two or three in 23 different science events. Events cover topics relating to core scientific disciplines such as biology, chemistry, physics, geology, and technology and may be build, study, or lab oriented in nature. Competitors acquire a deep understanding of scientific fields while developing valuable STEM skills and forming strong team bonds.

About Our Team

Since 1986, Centerville High School Science Olympiad has been a national powerhouse, obtaining 2 national titles, 20 state titles, and 8 additional top 3 finishes at the national tournament. We have dominated from coast to coast, beating out teams from all over the nation, ranging from New York to California.

Get Involved

Whether you're a student, parent, teacher, or member of the community,
your support is greatly appreciated

  • Join the Team

    CHS Students - Stay posted for updates in Fall 2023 or stop by Mrs. Herner's room, C113, for info.

    Middle Schoolers - Reach out to your point-of-contact on the Contact Page.

  • Donate

    All donations - corporate and personal, drive the success of our program. Everything that is raised goes directly into trip and material costs, allowing us to compete at the highest level. See our Donation Page for sponsor benefits and forms.

  • Lend a Hand

    If interested in potential event coach openings or invitational volunteer opportunities, please contact a head coach. All help is deeply valued.

  • Stay up to Date

    Stay tuned for announcements once the school year begins and check the website regularly so you don't miss a thing.

  • Support Our Sponsors

    Help us give back to our sponsors by utilizing their goods and services and thanking them for their support.

  • Spread the Word

    Reach out to friends and classmates about joining Science Olympiad. If you know of local businesses that are interested in sponsoring us, please reach out to a point-of-contact or send a sponsor form.
